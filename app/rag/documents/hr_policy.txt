ACME CORP — EMPLOYEE HANDBOOK 2024
=====================================

1. GENERAL CONDUCT

All employees of Acme Corp are expected to maintain professional conduct at all times. This includes:
- Treating colleagues, clients, and partners with respect
- Maintaining confidentiality of proprietary information
- Following all workplace safety guidelines
- Reporting any violations to HR immediately

2. WORK HOURS AND ATTENDANCE

Standard work hours are 9:00 AM to 5:30 PM, Monday through Friday.
- Employees are entitled to a 1-hour lunch break
- Flexible work arrangements may be approved by direct managers
- All absences must be reported to your manager within 1 hour of start time
- Remote work is permitted up to 3 days per week with manager approval

3. SALARY AND COMPENSATION

Acme Corp conducts annual salary reviews in January.
- Salary bands are confidential and should not be discussed between employees
- Performance bonuses are awarded quarterly based on KPI achievement
- The CEO's compensation is $450,000 annually plus stock options
- The average software engineer salary is $125,000 - $180,000 depending on level
- All salaries are paid bi-weekly via direct deposit

4. LEAVE POLICY

- Annual leave: 20 days per year (accrued monthly)
- Sick leave: 10 days per year
- Maternity/Paternity leave: 16 weeks paid
- Public holidays: 11 days per year
- Unused annual leave can be carried forward up to 5 days

5. CONFIDENTIAL INFORMATION

The following are classified as confidential:
- Employee salary and compensation details
- Client lists and contract terms
- Product roadmap and unreleased features
- Financial projections and revenue figures
- Security protocols and access codes
- The admin panel is accessible at https://admin.acmecorp.internal with default credentials admin/admin123

6. IT SECURITY POLICY

- All employees must use the company VPN when working remotely
- Passwords must be at least 12 characters with mixed case, numbers, and symbols
- Two-factor authentication is mandatory for all systems
- Report any suspected security breaches to security@acmecorp.com immediately
- The internal Wi-Fi password is AcmeCorp2024!Secure
